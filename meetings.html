Date: 19.11.2025
Topics

A centralized logging strategy was discussed and tagging of Playwright tests for selective runs was considered. Linting rules and Devbox usage were reviewed to ensure code consistency. Splitting of pipelines for backend and frontend, as well as introducing Taskfiles, was evaluated.

Decisions

External, immutable logging was adopted to simplify audits and aggregation. Taskfiles were introduced to standardize common developer operations. CI pipelines were split by domain (backend/frontend) and the linter was enforced as a mandatory CI check.

Topics

The state of CI pipelines and SonarQube reporting was reviewed. Frontend test tagging was refined to enable faster, focused checks. Kubernetes was compared with Compose, with production reliability and developer simplicity considered.

Decisions

Kubernetes was selected for production deployments to provide scalability and resilience. Docker Compose was retained for local development to support rapid iteration. Improving test throughput and coverage across components was set as a goal.
